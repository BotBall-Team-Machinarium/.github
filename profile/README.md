### Our fleet consists of two robots, each with a different mission but a common purpose.
## 1. Janitor's tasks
  - Collects ice
  - Pours ice into cups
  - Collects bottles
## 2. Bartender's tasks
  - Positions cups
  - Collects drinks
  - Pours drinks into cups
  - Transports cups to the beverage station
